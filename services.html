Our Services

We navigate the complex paperwork of the Indian financial system so you don't have to. Strictly non-advisory execution support.

Physical Shares to Demat

Full support for converting old physical share certificates into electronic Demat format. We handle the interaction with RTA (Registrar & Transfer Agents).

  • Name Correction & Affidavit drafting
  • Signature Mismatch resolution
  • Duplicate Share Certificate issuance
  • Transmission of shares (Death claims)

IEPF Claims

Recovering unclaimed dividends and shares from the Investor Education and Protection Fund (IEPF) Authority.

  • IEPF Form 5 Filing support
  • Document verification & submission
  • Tracking claim status with the Ministry

NRI Banking Support

Assisting Non-Resident Indians with banking operational hurdles without the need to travel to India.

  • Re-KYC & Account Reactivation
  • NRO/NRE Account Conversion
  • Capital Gains Statement generation
